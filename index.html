About This Portal

On the left side, you will find a list of navigation links. When you click on any link, the corresponding website will be displayed on the right side of the page. Below is a brief description of each link available in the navigation panel.

Navigation Links

1. Wikipedia

Wikipedia is a free online encyclopedia that anyone can edit. It contains millions of articles on a wide range of topics including science, history, geography, technology, culture, and more. It is one of the most visited websites in the world and is available in many languages.

2. JSS Science and Technology University

JSS Science and Technology University (formerly known as Sri Jayachamarajendra College of Engineering) is located in Mysuru, Karnataka, India. It is a well-known technical university that offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, technology, and science. The university is affiliated with various research and industry collaborations.

3. Google Scholar

Google Scholar is a freely accessible web search engine that indexes scholarly literature across many formats and disciplines. Students and researchers can use it to find academic papers, theses, books, conference papers, and technical reports from universities and research organizations worldwide.

4. GitHub

GitHub is a web-based platform used for version control and collaborative software development. It uses Git as the underlying version control system. Developers use GitHub to host their code, track changes, collaborate with others, and contribute to open-source projects. It is widely used by individual developers, teams, and companies around the world.

5. LeetCode

LeetCode is an online platform that provides coding challenges and problems to help programmers improve their coding skills. It is especially popular among students and software engineers who are preparing for technical interviews at top technology companies. The problems are categorized by difficulty level and topic, such as arrays, strings, trees, and dynamic programming.
